Since I was a child, I have been studying with interest in computers, and when I entered middle school, I thought that I wanted to have a computer-related job. I started studying in earnest. Also, I was interested in art, and since I have been doing it, I dream of being a UI/UX designer naturally. I got it. I have gained practical experience dealing with various programs based on ADOBE, and now I am studying with HTML and CSS in parallel.
